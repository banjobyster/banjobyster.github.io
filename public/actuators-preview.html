FIXTURE GALLERY · THE MACHINE'S STATIONS

The page is one system: data enters at the INTAKE breaker, is processed by the DEPLOY PIPELINE's knife switch, feeds each project through its PORT plug, syncs to the ARCHIVE datastore behind a latching kill button, and the last of it lights the EMAIL NEON on a slide lever at EOF. Every station is a control with a moving part; hover one and its tag proposes what a click will do. Click or Enter/Space flips it. FEED simulates the intake brownout; THEME checks both palettes. On the live page these same states are driven by the bysters through the fixture store.
